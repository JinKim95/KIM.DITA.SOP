How to Clean the Machine

These instructions will show you show to clean the machine effectively and safely

Do not immerse the motor base in water

Do not clean any of the parts with abrasive cleaners or hard implements

Cleaning the machine should be done after every use of the machine to ensure users stay healthy and the machine is not damaged

  1. Dissemble the machine by parts
  2. Submerge the mixing arm and lid in warm soapy water
  3. Clean thoroughly with a sponge
  4. Wipe clean the motor base with damp cloth
  5. Dry all parts thoroughly
  6. Leave parts on dry towel for next user
